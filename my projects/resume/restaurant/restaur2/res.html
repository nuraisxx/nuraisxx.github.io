About restaurant


Founded in 1984

LA COZZA INFURIATA is one of the oldest, family-run restaurants in London. The restaurant was established by Gino Santin in 1984 but is now run by his daughter Laura Santini, whose main focus is to take Santini into the future whilst maintaining the legacy her father created 36 years ago. Laura is among the world’s leading experts in flavour. Much of her passion for food came from her father Gino and her Italian ‘Nonna’. Laura is an award-winning author and has published four internationally acclaimed Cookbooks: Easy Tasty Italian, Flash Cooking: fit fast flavours for busy people, At Home with Umami and Pasta Perfect. The future and history of Santini coincide in our menu, curated by Laura herself, whilst incorporating signature dishes created by founder Gino Santin.
